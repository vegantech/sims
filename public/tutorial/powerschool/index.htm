Powerschool

Once you setup the reports, you can run them to generate the csv files as often as desired.

Setup Reports

Reports

Save each of these to your computer. You may need to right-click on them.

  • All Students in District (currently all users) Export
  • Enrollments
  • Schools
  • Students
  • Users (With usernames and emails)

Directions

  1. Login to powerschool admin site.
  2. Follow the Reports link.
  3. Click on the Engine tab.
  4. Press Choose File and select one of the rpt files you saved to your computer.
  5. Press Import.
  6. Press Back.
  7. Repeat steps 4,5,6 with each .rpt file.

Generate Reports

  1. Login to powerschool admin site.
  2. Follow the Reports link.
  3. Click on the Engine tab.
  4. Click on a report.
  5. Press Submit (you may have to scroll down.)
  6. Press Submit in the window or tab that has popped up
  7. You may close the new window or tab.
  8. Repeat steps 4,5,6 for each report
  9. You can now upload the csv files to SIMS (I recommend zipping them together first.)
